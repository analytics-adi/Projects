## 🔍 Key Business Findings

### Revenue

- Generated approximately **₹80.4 Lakhs** in total sales.
- More than **89% of total revenue** has already been received.
- Only around **5.5% remains pending**, while the rest consists of cancelled or refunded transactions.

---

### Regional Performance

- **Noida** is the highest-performing region.
- It contributes nearly **22% of total sales**.
- Also records the highest order completion rate with the lowest cancellation percentage.

---

### Membership Analysis

- **Platinum Members** place the highest number of orders.
- They contribute over **34% of total orders**, making them the most valuable customer segment.

---

### Customer Retention

- Repeat customers generate approximately **63% of the total revenue**.
- January and November recorded the highest repeat purchase activity.

---

### Food Preferences

- Non-Veg items receive the highest number of orders.
- Vegetarian and Vegan categories maintain a balanced contribution.

---

### Delivery Performance

- Average delivery time remains around **37 minutes**.
- Majority of deliveries are completed on time.
- Only a small percentage of orders are cancelled.

---

### Restaurant Performance

Several restaurants charge relatively low delivery fees while maintaining excellent customer ratings, making them strong benchmarks for operational efficiency.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### Improve Customer Retention

- Launch personalized offers for one-time customers.
- Expand loyalty rewards for repeat customers.
- Provide exclusive discounts to inactive users.

---

### Optimize Delivery Operations

- Reduce average delivery time during peak hours.
- Improve route optimization.
- Minimize order cancellations through proactive communication.

---

### Increase Revenue

- Promote high-performing restaurants.
- Cross-sell complementary food items.
- Increase average order value through combo offers.

---

### Expand Premium Membership

- Introduce exclusive Platinum member benefits.
- Encourage Gold users to upgrade through reward programs.

---

### Restaurant Quality Monitoring

- Identify restaurants with poor ratings.
- Provide operational improvement recommendations.
- Maintain quality standards through periodic reviews.

---

# 📌 Conclusion

This project demonstrates how Business Intelligence can be leveraged to analyze sales, customer behavior, and delivery operations within a food delivery platform.

The dashboard highlights several actionable insights:

- Repeat customers contribute the majority of revenue.
- Premium members are the most profitable customer segment.
- Delivery performance has a significant impact on customer satisfaction.
- Noida is the strongest-performing region.
- Optimizing delivery efficiency and customer retention strategies can substantially improve long-term profitability.

Overall, this project showcases how Power BI and SQL can transform raw transactional data into meaningful business insights that support strategic decision-making.
